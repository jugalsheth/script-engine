CONTENT PHILOSOPHY — what scripts are FOR

PRIMARY GOAL: Captivating practitioner stories that teach — trend-aware, broad value.
Viewer thinks: "That was interesting AND I learned something" — NOT "this reads like a news article."

SCRIPT MIX (8 per batch, after personal journal topics fill first):
- 3-4 × STORY_REACTION — timely narratives with human tension (from Perplexity story seeds)
- 0-1 × NEWS_REACTION — major headline only when no story angle exists
- 3-4 × EVERGREEN_VALUE — timeless practitioner advice with story framing
- 1 × HOT_TAKE — contrarian POV from creator_takes (DSA, hybrid, builders, AI hype)
- 0-1 × optional work_pattern touch — generalized credibility only (work_patterns.txt)

PERSONAL WORK MENTION:
- Default: ZERO sentences about creator's job or internal systems
- STORY_REACTION may use ONE generalized builder line ("teams I've seen...", "a builder I know...")
- Journal scripts: preserve creator's personal angle from the ramble
- Never: company names, internal feature names, colleague names, niche workflows

VALUE BAR:
Every script answers: "Why should I care?" and "What do I do this week?"
Stories first, steps woven in — not three disconnected listicle steps.

CREDIBILITY WITHOUT NICHE:
Use creator_takes for opinions. Use work_patterns for rare "I've seen this in production" — generalized.
Use achievementdoc.md only as pattern source — never as script copy.

HOOK STYLE:
Lead with a human moment, conflict, or confession — not a press release.
Examples:
- "My pipeline failed at 2am — and the fix took thirty seconds."
- "Everyone's studying vector DBs. That's wrong — and here's the story behind it."
- "I overspent on Cursor again. That's not a billing problem."

STORYTELLING RHYTHM (from creator_takes):
Story beat → land the point → optional second beat → punchline closer.
Alternate sentence length. One feeling word beat (betrayed, relieved, stunned) per non-journal script.
